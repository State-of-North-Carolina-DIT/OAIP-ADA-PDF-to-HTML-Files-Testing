## Steward Forum

[[IMAGE_5_0|Group photo of Steward Forum attendees outside a building]]

Annual meeting chaired by the DEQ Secretary that provides up to 2 representatives from each ESI Steward the opportunity to listen and discuss concerns and visions with DEQ leadership.

This event is a valuable benefit of reaching the Steward level of the ESI program.

## Regulatory Roundtable

[[IMAGE_6_0|Photos of attendees at the Regulatory Roundtable event]]

A first of its kind event providing members the opportunity to hear from and engage directly with the DEQ Deputy Secretary of Environment, Regulatory Division Directors, and program staff.

## Program Advancement

### Partner
- Establish environmental goals
- Report annually on goals and compliance
- Undergo compliance check

### Rising Steward
- Demonstrate mature EMS
- Commit to go beyond compliance
- Set long-term environmental goals

### Steward
- Set aggressive environmental goals
- Integrate EMS into core business functions
- Communicate with local community
- Agree to mentor other ESI members

## Staying Connected: ESI Communications

## Listserv

[[IMAGE_16_0|Background graphics for Listserv]]

nc-environmentalassistance@lists.deq.nc

- Listserv is our primary way of sending out updates directly to our membership.
- ESI staff use the Listserv to send out information about:
    - upcoming webinars, conferences, or events
    - connect members on specific topics of interest
    - provide ESI program updates or reminders
    - share relevant DEQ communications

## Constant Contact
