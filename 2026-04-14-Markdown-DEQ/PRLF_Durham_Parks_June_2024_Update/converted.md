# Ongoing Activities

1. Report Revisions and Field Marking – Field Markings were completed on April 
   16th. Revised soil cover evaluation reports (Lyon Park and Northgate Park) were 
   submitted to NCDEQ on 7/1/24. NCDEQ is currently reviewing the reports.

2. Additional Soil Cover Sampling – Final soil cover evaluation (all parks, and additional 
   areas for East Durham and East End) is complete. S&ME is currently drafting the 
   reports with a tentative draft report submittal date of 7/19/24 to NCDEQ for review.

3. Topographic/Boundary Surveys – Fieldwork is complete. S&ME has received the 
   topographic and boundary surveys for East Durham, Walltown, Northgate, East End Park 
   – Estimated delivery by 7/5/24 and Lyon – Estimated delivery by 7/9/24.

4. Site Construction Budget Estimates – Finalized on 6/18/24. S&ME will discuss the 
   budget estimates during the 7/15/24 Public Engagement Meeting.

5. Playground Material Removal and Sampling Contractor Selection/Pre-bid meeting – 
   Pre-bid meeting for the removal of the playgrounds was held on 5/29/24. S&ME is 
   awaiting results from the mulch TCLP samples and will send a report as soon as the 
   results are received.

6. Playground Fencing – Work plan was submitted to NCDEQ on 6/21/24 and was 
   approved. Seegars Fencing was awarded the project and will begin work on 7/17/24.

7. Playground Material Removal – Work plan 821RI-3 is underway. EVO was selected as 
   the remediation contractor. S&ME has asked EVO for a schedule. Once S&ME has the 
   schedule including the project duration, S&ME will provide the work plan to 
   NCDEQ. EVO has tentatively confirmed a start date of August 12th. Utility locating will 
   be conducted on 7/11/24

8. Public Meeting July 15, 2024 (4 - 5 pm). S&ME will attend this meeting and will be 
   prepared to discuss the DP-7 Construction Estimates and the revised DP-2 Reports.
