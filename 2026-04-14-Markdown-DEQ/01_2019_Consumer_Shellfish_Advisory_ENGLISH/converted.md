# CONSUMER ADVISORY

Eating raw, or undercooked *oysters, clams or mussels* may cause severe illness. People with the following conditions are at especially high risk: liver disease, alcoholism, diabetes, cancer, stomach or blood disorder, or weakened immune system. Ask your doctor if you are unsure of your risk. If you eat raw, or undercooked shellfish and become sick, see a doctor immediately.

N.C. DEPARTMENT OF ENVIRONMENTAL QUALITY  
DIVISION OF MARINE FISHERIES

Shellfish Sanitation Section

Failure to post this Consumer Advisory is a violation of the “Rules Governing the Sanitation of Restaurants and Other Foodhandling Establishments” and the “Rules Governing the Sanitation of Shellfish.” Violations of such rules are classified as a Class I misdemeanor under General Statute 130A-25.
