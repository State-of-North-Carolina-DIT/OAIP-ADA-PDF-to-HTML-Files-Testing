## AUDIO TROUBLESHOOTING

If you are not hearing any sound, try the following:

Make sure you are connected to the audio conference. When connected, a phone or headset icon should appear next to your name in Participants Panel.

Use the Speaker, Microphone, Camera setting under the Communicate menu, to make sure you have selected the correct speaker to use for audio. You may also adjust the audio volume from this screen.

Or you can select Audio Connection to use the Call Me function.
