# PROCLAMATION

## RE: POTS OPEN – ATLANTIC OCEAN AND INTERNAL COASTAL AND JOINT FISHING WATERS WEST AND SOUTH OF HIGHWAY 58 BRIDGE

**This proclamation supersedes proclamation PT-1-2023, dated April 28, 2023. This proclamation describes areas open to crab, eel, fish, and shrimp pots in the Atlantic Ocean and Internal Coastal and Joint Fishing Waters from west and south of the Highway 58 Bridge to the North Carolina/South Carolina state line. This proclamation will expire at 11:59 PM, November 30, 2024, unless superseded by another proclamation.**

Kathy B. Rawls, Director, Division of Marine Fisheries, hereby announces that effective at **12:01 A.M., Wednesday, May 1, 2024 until 11:59 PM, Saturday, November 30, 2024**, the following restrictions shall apply to crab, eel, fish and shrimp pots in Internal Coastal and Joint Fishing Waters:

## IT SHALL BE UNLAWFULLTO USE POTS, EXCEPT IN AREAS DESCRIBED BELOW:

1. ATLANTIC OCEAN – Statewide
1. Designated primary nursery areas in all Internal Coastal and Joint Waters west and south  
   of the Highway 58 Bridge at Emerald Isle which are listed in N.C. Marine Fisheries  
   Commission Rule 15A NCAC 03R .0103. For more information about designated nursery  
   areas visit the [NCDMF Interactive Map](https://ncdenr.maps.arcgis.com/home/webmap/viewer.html?useExisting=1&layers=671ea4b8ffc0456fa64b3d02d16e31fa). 
1. **All Internal Coastal and Joint Fishing Waters west and south of the Highway 58  
   Bridge at Emerald Isle which are closed by N.C. Marine Fisheries Commission Rule  
   or Division of Marine Fisheries proclamation for crab trawling and taking shrimp  
   with nets.**
1. All Shellfish Management Areas which have been designated by proclamation.

1. **GENERAL INFORMATION**

      Fisheries Commission Rules 15A NCAC 03H .0103, 03J .0301, and 03K .0103.
   1. It is unlawful to violate the provisions of any proclamation issued by the Fisheries  
      Director under their delegated authority pursuant to N.C. Marine Fisheries  
      Commission Rule 15A NCAC 03H .0103.
   1. The intent of this proclamation is to manage the pot fishery in the Atlantic Ocean  
      and the Internal Coastal and Joint Fishing Waters of the state from the Highway  
      58 Bridge west and south to the North Carolina/South Carolina state line.
   1. In accordance with N.C. Marine Fisheries Commission Rule 15A NCAC  
      03J .0301(b)(1), it shall be unlawful to use pots in any navigation channel marked
