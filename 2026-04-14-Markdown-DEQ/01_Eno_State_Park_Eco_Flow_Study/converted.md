# The Next Step: Eno River Demonstration Project

## NC Ecological Flows Science Advisory Board

[[IMAGE_2_0|Slide background]]

## Eno River State Park Ecological Flow Study

- Data collected 7/1986 – 6/1988 at flows ranging from 1.9 to 234 cfs
- Existing model of physical/ hydraulic conditions will be used
- Habitat suitability indices will be updated with additional aquatic species and guilds

[[IMAGE_3_0|Slide background]]

## Eno River State Park Ecological Flow Study

- Various flow management alternatives will be evaluated for habitat effects.
- A second Eno River study site farther upstream at Hillsborough will also be evaluated using a similar approach.
- If this approach proves workable, there are other existing flow vs. habitat study sites on other types of streams that could also be analyzed.

## Eno River State Park Ecological Flow Study - Site Characteristics

- Drainage Area = 99.4 square miles
- Mean Annual Flow ~ 86.4 cfs
- 7Q10 ~ 2.6 cfs
- September median flow ~ 11.2 cfs
- Wetted perimeter method flow recommendation = 19.4 cfs
- Note: All photos in the following slides are taken from the left bank as you face upstream
